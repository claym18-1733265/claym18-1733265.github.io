Harrison Mills & Clayton Knight

ODESZA is originally from Seattle, Washington. The two members of the electronic music band, ODESZA, are Harrison Mills and Clayton Knight. They attended Western Washington University, and became ODESZA shortly before they graduated. They are now one of the most well know electronic music bands in the world.
